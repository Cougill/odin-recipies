Chocolate Strawberry Cheesecake

Crust

The ingredients that will be required for the crust:

  • Cooking Spray
  • A box of chocolate wafer cookies (9 Ounces)
  • 5 Tablespoons - Unsalted Butter (Melted)
  • 3 Tablespoons - Granulated Sugar

Cheesecake

The ingredients that will be required for the cheesecake:

  • 1 Cup - Chopped fresh strawberries
  • 5 Packs - Cream Cheese, Softened (8 Ounces)
  • 1/2 Cup - Granulated Sugar
  • 3 Tablespoons - All-Purpose Flour
  • 5 Large Eggs
  • 2 Large Egg Yolks
  • 2 Teaspoons - Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Teaspoon - Lemon Zest
  • 1 Tablespoon Freshly Squeezed Lemon Juice
  • 2 Drops - Pink Gel Food Coloring

Ganache

The ingredients that will be required for the chocolate ganache:

  • 1 Cup - Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 1 Bar - Semisweet Chocolate, Finely chopped (4 Ounces)
  • 1 Bar - Bittersweet Chocolate, Finely chopped (4 Ounces)
  • 10 Strawberries (Whole and Halved), For garnish
