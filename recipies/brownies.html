Brownies

Brownie Ingredients

The ingredients that will be required for the brownies:

  • Butter
  • White Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vannila Extract
  • Cocoa Powder
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Salt
  • Baking Powder

Frosting Ingredients

The ingredients that will be required for the frosting:

  • Butter
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Honey
  • Vannila Extract
  • Confectioners' Sugar
